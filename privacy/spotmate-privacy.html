SpotMate Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Last updated: March 03, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes Our policies and procedures on the collection, use, and disclosure of Your information when You use the Service and tells You about Your privacy rights and how the law protects You.

At SpotMate, we prioritize your privacy. Currently, we do not collect any personal data. The only features that would involve data collection are iCloud sync and syncing with the Apple Contacts app, which are not yet implemented in the app.

Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

Currently, SpotMate does not collect any personal data from its users. The app does not collect any personal information unless the iCloud sync or Apple Contacts syncing features are enabled, which are not yet implemented.

Information Regarding iCloud Sync and Apple Contacts Sync

Once implemented, iCloud sync will allow SpotMate users to sync their data across devices using the same Apple ID. This feature would involve syncing contact data between SpotMate and iCloud, but at present, it is not yet available.

Additionally, we may need to request access to the Apple Contacts app to sync contacts between the app and the contacts stored on your device. This feature would allow for easier management of contacts within SpotMate, but like iCloud sync, it has not yet been implemented.

Please note that these features will only collect and sync data if they are enabled. Until then, SpotMate does not access or collect any personal data.

Use of Your Personal Data

Since SpotMate currently does not collect any personal data, we do not use any personal data in any way. When the iCloud sync and Apple Contacts syncing features are implemented, we will use the data solely for the purposes of syncing and managing your contacts. No other personal data will be used by the app at this time.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app, the addition of new features (such as iCloud sync and Apple Contacts syncing), or legal requirements. Any changes will be reflected in an updated version of this Privacy Policy, which will be made available within the app and on our website.

As we do not currently collect any personal data, there will be no need for us to notify users directly about changes to this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information. The most recent version of this Privacy Policy will always be available in the app and on our website.
